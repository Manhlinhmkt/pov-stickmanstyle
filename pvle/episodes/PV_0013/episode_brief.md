## CONFIRMED OUTLINE

### HOOK
- News calls it digital gold. You stare at your coffee. You know who built it. You can't say a word.
- 1 million coins sitting untouched - worth tens of billions - never moved
- "You created digital gold. Then you stood up and walked away."

### PHASE_EARLY (The Obsession - 2007-2008)
- Late nights, coding alone - nobody asked you to fix money
- But you can't stop thinking about it
- If you fail, nobody knows. If you succeed, everything changes.
- 9-page whitepaper - Oct 31 - sent to cryptography mailing list - mostly silence
- Register bitcoin.org - fake name, fake address - only the code is real
- Fake profile: born April 5, 1975 - the date gold was banned + the year gold ownership was restored
- You live alone with this secret - no colleagues, no mentor, no one
- You choose anonymity not from fear - but because you know this must be bigger than any name

### PHASE_CONFLICT (Gold Takes Shape - Jan 2009 - 2010)
- Jan 3, 2009: mine Genesis Block - embed headline "Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks"
- Send 10 BTC to Hal Finney - first transaction in history - worth exactly nothing
- Then people start using it. Not because they trust you. Because they trust the system.
- That is exactly what you wanted. And that is exactly what will erase you.
- 10,000 BTC for 2 pizzas - nobody realizes they are holding gold
- Mine ~1 million BTC - worth nothing then - will become the largest untouched fortune in history
- WikiLeaks accepts Bitcoin - you write: "kicking a hornet's nest too early" - attention brings danger
- You begin to understand: either you leave, or you destroy it
- The project is only truly decentralized when its creator walks away

### PHASE_CRISIS (Walking Away - Apr 2011)
- Hand over source code + network alert key to Gavin Andresen
- Apr 23: email Mike Hearn - "I've moved on to other things. It's in good hands."
- Apr 26: final email to Gavin - "stop calling me a mysterious shadowy figure"
- Apr 27: Gavin announces he will present Bitcoin at the CIA
- You read that line. No panic. No running.
- You just sit there a little longer than usual - with the thing you are about to leave behind
- Then you close the laptop. No one forced you. No one chased you. You stand up and walk away.
- 1 million BTC sits still in the blockchain - that is not forgetting - that is a silent promise

### PHASE_RESOLUTION (The Person Who Does Not Exist - 2011-present)
- Bitcoin: $1 to $100 to $1,000 to $10,000 to $60,000+ - digital gold becomes real
- 1 million BTC = tens of billions USD - you are the richest person in the world - on paper
- The world keeps trying to find you. You keep not existing.
- Every few years, someone becomes you. And every time - you let them.
- Dorian Nakamoto ambushed by reporters - not you
- Craig Wright claims in court - court rejects - not you
- Hal Finney dies 2014 - the first person who believed in you - you cannot attend his funeral
- You sit in a cafe - someone says "Bitcoin is the future" - you nod politely
- Digital gold is everywhere. The person who created it is nowhere.

### CALLBACK_CLOSE
- Cafe - old laptop - Bitcoin price on screen
- Stranger next to you: "I wonder where Satoshi is now"
- You take a sip of coffee. Say nothing.
- "The price of creating digital gold - is that you can never claim it as yours."
