- You respond on the platform you own  -  tweet at 2AM

- You didn't reform government. You proved you *could* reform government.
- **"Difference between builder and ruler: builder makes things run. Ruler makes things obey."**

- Your daughter talks about you in the press  -  under her new name, not yours
- Mom calls  -  you don't answer
- You look at the launchpad through the trailer window

- You escaped South Africa. Escaped poverty. Escaped marriages. Escaped gravity.
- **"Maybe escape is the only thing you actually know how to do."**

### CALLBACK_CLOSE
